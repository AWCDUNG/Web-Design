Dangerous Goods Classification

1

Explosives

  • Group 1.1: Explosive substances

2

  • Group 2.1: Flammable gases

4

  • Group 4.1: Flammable solids, self-reactive substances
  • Group 4.2: Substances liable to spontaneous combustion
  • Group 4.3: Substances which emit flammable gases when in contact with water
5

  • Group 5.1: Oxidizing substances
  • Group 5.2: Organic peroxides
6

Toxic substances

  • Group 6.1: Toxic substances
  • Group 6.2: Infectious substances
7

Radioactive materials

Radioactive materials as defined by the Atomic Energy Law

8

Corrosive substances

Substances with high corrosive properties

9

Miscellaneous dangerous substances

Dangerous substances and goods not belonging to the above 8 classes
